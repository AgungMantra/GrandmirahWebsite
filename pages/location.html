Grand Mirah Boutique Hotel

Grand Mirah Boutique Hotel is located in the heart of Denpasar and is close to some of the island’s most historical and culturally inspiring sites. It is within easy walking distance to Puputan Square, Jagatnatha Temple and Bali’s Provincial State Museum.

Nearby places of interest include Gajah Mada Street and Pasar Badung,a traditional marketplace selling everything from fresh food to ceremonial items, clothing and souvenirs. There is also Pasar Burung where different species of birds and live animals are sold.

Grand Mirah Boutique Hotel is approximately 30 minutes by car from Bali’s Ngurah Rai International Airport. It is strategically positioned to enjoy local cuisine and there are many small food stalls in the immediate area.
