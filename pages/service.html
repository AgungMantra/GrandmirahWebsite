Grand Mirah Service or Facilities

Grand Mirah Boutique Hotel is staffed by a small but professional team of staff who are on hand to provide sincere hospitality with a warm Balinese smile. All essential services and facilities are available to ensure that every stay is a memorable experience.

Description

Lobby Lounge

Located on the ground floor, the Lobby Lounge is a social space to sit in comfort with family members or business colleagues. A full menu of selected beverages and alcoholic drinks is also available to enjoy whilst waiting for transportation.

Swimming Pool

Designed for the pleasure of in-house guests, the swimming pool is a tropical environment edged by a timber deck and a vertical garden. There is a shallow section for children to play safely as well as reclining sun beds for heightened relaxation.

Grand Mirah Hotel

Red Stone Coffee House

Everyone is welcome at Red Stone Coffee House, a stylish spot to eat, drink and meet in the heart of Denpasar. It serves light bites, pastries and a flavoursome menu of Indonesian cuisine throughout the day. The decor is highlighted by a collection of prints documenting local history.
